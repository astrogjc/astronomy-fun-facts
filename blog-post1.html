Exploring the Phases of the Moon

What Are Moon Phases?

The Moon goes through several phases as it orbits the Earth. These phases include:

  • New Moon
  • Waxing Crescent
  • First Quarter
  • Waxing Gibbous
  • Full Moon
  • Waning Gibbous
  • Last Quarter
  • Waning Crescent

The Science Behind Moon Phases

Moon phases occur due to the relative positions of the Earth, Moon, and Sun. As the Moon travels around Earth, different parts of it are illuminated by sunlight, creating the varying appearances we see from Earth.

Fun Facts

  • During a Full Moon, the Moon appears about 14% larger and 30% brighter than usual!
  • “Once in a Blue Moon” refers to the second Full Moon in a calendar month, a rare event!
